About

Kolkata(formally culcutta) is the capital of india's state west bengal. Founded as an East India Company trading post, it was the capital of india under the british raj from 1773-1911. Today it is known for its grand colonial architecture, art galleries and cultural festivals. It's also home of mother house, headquaters of the Missionaries of Charity, founded by Mother Teresa, whose tomb is on site.
